Mallard Duck

Mallards are large ducks, bigger than a crow, but smaller than a goose. Mallards have distinct colors depending on whether they are male or female. The males have an iridescent-green head and a yellow bill, with a gray body. The females and juveniles are brown, and have brown bills.
