Rooster

The rooster. The bird of the morning. A powerful symbol in nearly every culture. The epitomy of avian grace and might. Some would say the rooster came down from the heavens to grace the mortal realm with its infinite beauty and wisdom. It's call is said to bring everlasting interal peace to all those who hear it. It is said when the golden rooster crows, a new universe is born.
